Night Vision Live

Every so often, NVL produces, promotes and executes ticketed live music events. NVL has a proven record of cutting edge talent bookings, tasteful venue selection, quality production value and highly targeted event marketing. Night Vision Live alum include Grammy-nominated producers, international powerhouse DJ’s, viral music sensations and top tier underground talent. Night Vision Live events promise cutting-edge music in a beautiful, intimate setting, and are always a one time only experience.

Night Vision Residency

The Night Vision crew hosts a traveling club residency here in New York City. This Summer, enjoy our bi-monthly club night every second and fourth Thursday at "The303", a brand new club on Broome Street geared toward the true music heads of downtown New York. The Night Vision residency has been home to iconic clubs such as Lil Charlies, Le Baron, and Le Bain at the Standard Hotel. Our traveling residency is the perfect happening for our local network of artists, young professionals, music fans and students to meet, mingle, and celebrate! Resident DJ’s Lenvi & Don Clark supply the tunes every week, providing cutting edge dance music sounds from around the world.
